Welcome to
The State Labour Institute

The State Labour Institute conducts various training programmes, seminars, workshops for the government officers, trade union leaders etc. Besides, it conducts a one year Post Graduate Diploma Course in Human Resource Development & Labour Welfare and Advanced Certificate Course in Construction Safety. It also publishes a quarterly magazine 'Shramik Barta'
