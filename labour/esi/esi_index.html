ESI (MB) Scheme

ESI MB Scheme, West Bengal is the key facilitator for rendering the medical services under the ESI Scheme in this State. The Employees' State Insurance Scheme was introduced under the Employees' State Insurance Act, 1948 as a Social Security measure with the objective of providing certain benefits to employees in case of sickness, maternity and employment injury etc.
