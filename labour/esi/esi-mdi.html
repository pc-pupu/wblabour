ESI Man Power Development Society (ESI MPDS)

The following courses are being run under the Man Power Development Society:

GNM Course under 'Nurses Training Centre', Manicktala & 'Nursing Training School', Sealdah:

Nurses Training Centre, E.S.I Hospital, Manicktala and Nursing Training School, E.S.I. Hospital Sealdah are two important Training Institutes for the Nurses being run the ESI Directorate. These Nursing Training Institutions conduct 3 ½ years General Nursing and Midwifery (GNM) courses with total intake of 90 trainee nurses (50 intake for NTC at Manicktala and 40 intake for NTS at Sealdah).

As per Indian Nursing Council, student nurses have to undergo 6 months internship after completion of 3rd year Examination and are paid Internship stipend. All facilities including Hostel, study materials, books and models, charts, instruments, sound system, computers and different types of furniture are provided.

B.Sc Nursing Course under College of Nursing, Asansol

A College of Nursing in Asansol for B Sc Nursing is also operational with an intake of 40 students per uear which at the moment is provisionally run by MPDS. A new building with hostel facilities for the same is nearing completion.

Diploma in Dialysis Technology (DDT)

Diploma in Dialysis Technology course is an important paramedical course run by Nephrology Department, ESI Hospital, Manicktala.

Optometry Course (D Opto)

Kamarhati ESI Hospital has started two years integrated Optometry training course

Diploma Medical Laboratory Technician (DMLT)

The ESI Institute of Pain management, in Sealdah ESI started off as a pain clinic has grown to be a major referral centre for Pain Management A basic gait analysis lab has been also inaugurated on 05.09.2014 by the Hon’ble MIC Department Labour, and is upgraded from time to time for its research works and evaluation of chronic low back pain as well as cancer patients with chronic pain.It also offers both training for Fellowship in Pain Management and research related works.

IT Enablement:

ESI Directorate web site

In order to improve two ways sharing of data, Web site for Directorate of ESI (MB) Scheme has been developed and the web site went live on the 2nd of January'09. The address being: www.esiwb.gov.in. The site has the detailed information ESI(MB) Scheme, of all the ESI hospitals and Dispensaries in West Bengal.

Project Dhanwantri

The Dhanwantri project is an application for the health service personnel and is the largest IT enablement project in public sector all over India and is being implemented in this state to make the entire system and functioning of all ESI hospitals and Dispensaries a 'Paperless' one.

Introduction of Ambulance on hire basis:

All the ESI Hospitals have been instructed to deploy Ambulances on hire basis as per rate fixed by the Transport Department of Govt. of W.B. All thirteen (13) ESI Hospitals have already deployed three (3 nos) Ambulances each on hire for the benefit of the I.Ps and their families.

Up-gradation/Improvements of the ESI Hospitals:

It has become a continuous practice to enhance and promote new service facilities under ESI healthcare plans and for last few years ESI Corporation took various Schemes which has been already taken up for the facelift and up- gradation and improvement of ESI Hospital to make it modern and up to date.

Future Plans:

  1. Completion of ongoing construction work for new 100 bedded hospital at Haldia and Siliguri.
  2. Extension of Dialysis unit at Asansol ESI Hospital.
  3. Upgradation of BSc Nursing College at Asansol ESI Hospital to M Sc Nursing College.
  4. Upgradation of GNM training courses at NTC Manicktala and NTS Sealdah to B Sc Nursing Colleges
  5. Setting up of Oncology Board at Sealdah ESI
  6. Establishment of newer diagnostic facilities [Endoscopy and Echocardiography with Colour Doppler etc.] at different ESI Hospitals.
  7. Completion of Dialysis Unit in Uluberia ESI
  8. Setting up of specialized Burns Unit in some ESI hospitals
  9. Relocation of NTS Sealdah to newly renovated premises
  10. Completion of extention work in Baltikuri, Durgapur and Asansol ESI Hospitals
  11. Coverage of ESI services to all districts of West Bengal under PIP
  12. Starting of the completed Cath Lab in Manicktala ESI
