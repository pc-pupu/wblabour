Vision - Mission

Vision

  • To be a premier Institute of excellence for capacity building of Labour Administrators, Workers (including Trade Unions) and Employers
  • Providing skilled personnel for working in the field of Industrial Relations

 

Mission

  • Capacity building of all stakeholders for better performance of the individual and Organization
  • Conduct research on Labour related issues
  • Publish journals on Labour related issues
