The Scheme and its Objectives

Overview of ESI Scheme

The Employees' State Insurance Scheme was introduced under the Employees' State Insurance Act, 1948 as a Social Security measure. Atpresent it is the largest Social Security Scheme in the country.

Apart from Industriesâ€”large and small, hotels, restaurants, theatres, newspaper establishments, motor or transport establishments, shops, schools, and other establishments employing 10 or more persons have been brought under the purview of the Scheme. The present upper ceiling of wages for coverage of any employee under the Scheme is Rs. 21,000/- per month. Recently private health care Institutions and medical diagnostic centres have also come under the preview.

The whole Scheme is administered by the Employees' State Insurance Corporation (ESIC) and the State Government. The Medical Benefits and various other benefits including cash benefits are provided under the Scheme. In terms of an agreement with the E.S.I. Corporation the Government of West Bengal is entrusted for administering the E.S.I. Medical Benefit Scheme for the Insured Persons and their families under the Adminstrative control of the Labour Department.

Objectives

To provide for certain benefits to Employees like Medical Benefits, Sickness benefit (in the form of financial assistance), Maternity Benefit, Disablement Benefit and Dependents Benefit and few other related benefits to the employees and their families in the organized sector.

The ESI(MB) Scheme is administered by the ESI Directorate Gov.WB and ESI Corporation. Medical Benefits and various other benefits including cash benefits are provided under the Scheme to the Insured Persons and their families with family size 3.8 (three point eight) in the State of West Bengal.

From July 2019 the ESI contribution of 4% of the annual salary is shared by the employer (3.25%) and the employee(0.75%)

As per agreement between the E.S.I. Corporation and the State Government the expenditure for running the E.S.I. (M.B) Scheme is to be shared between the E.S.I. Corporation and the State Government in the ratio of 7:1 subject to overall ceiling of the expenditure per I.P/per annum as may be fixed by the E.S.I. Corporation from time to time. The present ceiling of expenditure of Rs.3000/- per I.P./per annum was fixed by the E.S.I. Corporation w.e.f. 5.12.16.

At present there are 19,22,090 number of IPs (as on 31-03-2019). Total number of beneficiaries is approximately 92 Lakhs.

Administrative Setup

The Employees' State Insurance Scheme is administered by a corporate body called the Employees' State Insurance Corporation (ESIC), which has members representing employers, employees, the Central Government, State Governments, members from medical profession and the Parliament.

The Medical Benefit Scheme is administered through the Directorate of Employees' State Insurance (M.B) Scheme under the Administrative Control of the Labour Department. The Director, ESI (M.B) Scheme is the Head of the Organization. He is assisted by the Officers in the rank of Joint Directors, Deputy Directors, Assistant Directors, Administrative Medical Officer, Additional A.M.O. and other Officers and Staff.

There are 13 Hospitals, 59 SDs (Including 18 Tie up SDs), Central Medical Store and 3 Rajya Bima Oushadhalayas which are manned by Medical Officers and staff under ESI(M.B.) Scheme.
